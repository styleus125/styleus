Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At Styleus, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using Styleus, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — name, email address, phone number, and password (stored as a secure hash) when you register.
  • Listing information — titles, descriptions, images, and pricing you provide when creating product or service listings.
  • Professional profile — name, address, phone, and area of work if you register as a professional.
  • Order information — items purchased, amounts, and shipping addresses provided during checkout.
  • Usage data — pages visited and session activity, used to improve the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain your account and listings.
  • To process orders and facilitate communication between buyers and sellers.
  • To display approved professional profiles in the Professionals directory.
  • To send you important service notifications (e.g., order updates, listing approvals).
  • To detect and prevent fraud or misuse of the platform.
  • To improve our services based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared only in these limited circumstances:

  • With buyers/sellers — contact details (e.g., phone number) you choose to display on your listing or professional profile are visible to other users.
  • With payment processors — payment information is handled by third-party processors (e.g., Stripe, PhonePe) and is never stored on our servers.
  • As required by law — if compelled by a court order or government authority.

4. Cookies & Session Data

We use essential session cookies to keep you logged in and to protect forms against cross-site request forgery (CSRF). We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features (such as login) will not function without them.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please contact us via WhatsApp at +91 9958808898. We will process your request within 30 days.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including password hashing, CSRF protection, and encrypted connections (HTTPS). However, no system is completely secure — please use a strong, unique password and do not share your credentials.

7. Children's Privacy

Styleus is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Styleus after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please reach out to us via WhatsApp at +91 9958808898.
