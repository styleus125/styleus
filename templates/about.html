About Us

Last updated: May 2026

Who We Are

Styleus is a community-first marketplace based in India, built to make buying, selling, and discovering local talent effortless. Whether you're shopping for new or pre-loved items, looking for a trusted professional, or listing your own products and services, Styleus brings it all together in one place.

Our Mission

We believe commerce should be personal. Our mission is to connect buyers and sellers in a trusted environment, support local professionals, and give everyone — from individuals to small businesses — a platform to grow. We keep things simple, transparent, and community-focused.

What We Offer

  • 1 Shop — Browse curated physical and digital products listed by Styleus.
  • 2 User Deals — Buy and sell pre-loved items listed by community members.
  • 3 Services — Access professional services offered through the platform.
  • 4 Professionals — Discover and connect with verified local professionals and businesses.
